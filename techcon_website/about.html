Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small gathering of 200 developers in San Francisco. What started as a one-day event has grown into the world's leading technology conference, attracting over 15,000 attendees from 80+ countries in 2023.

Over the years, we've expanded to include workshops, hackathons, and dedicated tracks for AI, cybersecurity, web development, and cloud computing.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower the next generation of technology leaders by providing a platform where groundbreaking ideas are shared, collaborations are born, and innovation thrives. We believe in inclusive, accessible, and forward-thinking technology that benefits everyone.
